Squats

The Barbell Squat targets the main muscle group Quadriceps.
but also targets the Hamstrings, Glutes and Calves


















Deadlifts

The Barbell Deadlift targets the main muscle group Lower Back.
But also targets the Calves, Forearms, Glutes, Hamstrings, Lats,
Middle back, Quadriceps and Traps.


















PullUps

The PullUp targets the main muscle group Lats.
But also targets Biceps and Middle Back


















Front Squats

The Front Squat targets the main muscle group Quadriceps.
But also targets Hamstrings, Glutes and Calves


















Barbell Curls

The Barbell Curl targets the main muscle group Bicep.
But also targets ForeArms


















Hammer Curls

The Hammer Curl targets the main muscle group Bicep.


















Bicep Curls

The Bicep Curl targets the main muscle group Bicep.
But also targets ForeArms


















Lateral Raises

The Lateral Raise targets the main muscle group Side Delts.
